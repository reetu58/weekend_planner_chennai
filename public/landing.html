Built for Chennai weekends

Every feature exists because the city has its own rules.

🚦
Departure-time aware routing
Enter when you leave. The planner adjusts stop order and travel gaps based on typical Chennai traffic patterns — so you don't end up stuck on OMR at 6 PM.
🕐
Only suggests open places
Every place is checked against your arrival time. No temple suggestions at 9 PM. No cafe-at-7-AM that opens at 11. What's in your plan is actually open.
🍜
Food placed at the right time
Breakfast in your first slot if you're heading out at 8 AM. Dinner suggestion at the end if you're going for an evening out. Food stops are timed, not just listed.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Group-aware suggestions
Couple? You'll get rooftop cafes and beach walks. Family with kids? Parks and family restaurants only. Solo? Off-beat spots and galleries. Your group shapes every pick.
📍
Proximity-first chaining
Places aren't just top-rated — they're near each other. After Marina Beach, you'll get Mylapore options, not Porur. Less driving, more doing.
🌙
Nightlife done right
Nightlife suggestions only appear for evening plans. Temples stay in morning and afternoon plans. The app understands Chennai's timing, so you don't have to.
